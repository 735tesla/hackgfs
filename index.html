What?

A hackathon is a timed event where developers from all over combine their creativity and enthusiasm to build something awesome! "Hacks" are simply the products built, and have nothing to do with Hollywood's idea of "hacking".

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are most of the essential questions that are usually asked about hackathons. If you have a specific question, please feel free to contact us!

What is hackGFS?

hackGFS is the first hackathon run by students from Germantown Friends School in Philadelphia. A hackathon is a social event where you are given 24 hours to build something dope while meeting new people and expanding your knowledge.

Who can attend hackGFS?

Anyone currently enrolled in high school can apply to attend hackGFS. Unfortunately, high school graduates cannot participate as a hacker, but you can be a mentor and help others!

How much does it cost?

Free! hackGFS is a free event that will provide you with meals, snacks, classic hackathon swag like shirts and sunglasses. Unfortunately, we will not be providing travel reimbursement for this event.

How do teams work?

Teams can be no larger than 4 developers, but can be groups of 2, 3, or you can work by yourself! Upon reviewing applications we will post a link to a facebook group of accepted applicants so you can meet people attending.

What if I don't know how to code?

That's fine! hackGFS is an event for people of all skillsets. We will have workshops throughout the event so you can learn from experienced developers.

Can I start my hack before the event?

Nope! Any hack you will be submitting for prizes and review from judges must be started after our kickoff for fairness.
